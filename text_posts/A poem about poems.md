What I used to think about poetry:
    You cut the whole into pieces
    Discard some, keep the rest
    And pretend the rest describe
    A bigger whole than the one from
    Which it came.

What I think about poetry now:
    You need to draw something
    But you cannot bear to look at it
    So you trace the outline
    Of everything it isn't.
    You wish another person would see your drawing
    And recognize
    The thing that isn't there.
